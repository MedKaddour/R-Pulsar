Platform

Overview

R-Pulsar is built upon data producers and consumers profiles [PAISE2019]. The process of placing functions and operators is coordinated amongst a federation of Rendez-vous Points that dynamically links producers and consumers. The rule-based engine uses run-time information to determine the most appropriate placement according to system and resources capabilities.
